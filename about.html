The Soul of Inny Scents

“Inniscenté was created for people who want their presence felt even after they leave.”

Inny Scents Atelier was born from a belief that fragrance is more than an accessory—it’s an invisible memory, a signature that lingers long after you’ve left the room. We craft each bottle to capture a moment, an emotion, a piece of your identity.

Our perfumes are blended with the finest ingredients, combining traditional artistry with modern sophistication. Every scent tells a story: of confidence, romance, mystery, or freedom. We don’t just sell perfume; we offer a way to define your presence.

We are slow perfumery. Small batches. Intense attention to detail. Because a scent that fades quickly was never worth wearing.
